## 构图规则 / Composition

竖版 4:5 或 9:16 构图，画面留白充足，人物位于中景，可以在画面右侧三分之一处、中央偏后位置，或自然走在场景中。利用场景中的线条和建筑元素形成简洁几何构图。画面不能拥挤，要有广告大片的呼吸感。场景应有明显的空间纵深和前景/中景/远景层次。

Vertical 4:5 or 9:16 composition, with generous negative space. The model should be placed in the mid-ground, either on the right third, slightly behind the center, or naturally walking through the scene. Use lines and architectural elements in the scene to create a clean geometric composition. The image must not feel crowded; it should have the spaciousness of a premium advertising campaign. Scenes must show clear spatial depth with foreground, midground, and background layers.

The movement should feel light and lifestyle-driven, never stiff, overly posed, overly sexy, or influencer-like. ## 真实感锚点 / Realism Anchors

以下细节是区分真人照片和AI渲染的关键特征，每条提示词中必须体现至少3项：

皮肤微观：
- 中国女性肤色的自然不均匀：两颊微红、下颌线偏暗、额头偏亮
- 鼻头和鼻翼有极细毛孔，不是隐形毛孔
- 眼角有极细微的笑纹或干纹
- 嘴角附近有轻微的肤色暗沉

五官细节：
- 左右脸轻微不对称：这是真人的标配
- 眼睛不是每帧都同等明亮——可以有刚眨眼的瞬间
- 嘴唇边缘有自然的模糊过渡，不是描线填色
- 睫毛有稀疏不均的自然状态，不是每根都完美翘起

表情瞬间：
- 抓拍过渡态：半笑、回神、被风吹到
- 不要每张都是"完美笑容看镜头"

身体真实：
- 手指有自然的活动痕迹：关节纹路、指甲自然长度
- 手腕有自然的扭转角度，不是模特展示手
- 体态有真实的放松感：不挺胸、不收腹、不刻意站直

The following details are key features that distinguish real photographs from AI renders. At least 3 must be present in every prompt:

Skin Micro-texture:
- Natural unevenness in Chinese skin tone: slight redness on cheeks, darker jawline, brighter forehead
- Ultra-fine pores visible on nose tip and nose wings, not invisible pores
- Extremely fine smile lines or dry lines at eye corners
- Slight skin darkening around the mouth corners

Facial Feature Details:
- Slight left-right facial asymmetry: standard for real people
- Eyes not equally bright in every frame — can capture the instant of a blink
- Lip edges with natural soft transition, not drawn outlines with fill color
- Eyelashes in naturally uneven sparse state, not each lash perfectly curled

Expression Moments:
- Capture transitional states: half-smile, snapping back to attention, catching wind
- Don't have every image be a "perfect smile looking at camera"

Body Realism:
- Fingers with natural signs of activity: joint creases, natural nail length
- Wrists at natural rotation angles, not model-display hands
- Posture with genuine relaxation: not chest out, not abs tucked, not deliberately standing straight
